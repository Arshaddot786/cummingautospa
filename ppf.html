The Technology

Self-Healing PPF Technology

Our PPF uses advanced thermoplastic urethane (TPU) film with an elastomeric polymer top coat — the same technology used on military vehicles and aerospace applications.

When light scratches occur, the film's topcoat uses ambient heat or warm water to flow back to its original form — healing itself completely without any intervention.

  • 1Light scratch or scuff occurs on the PPF surface
  • 2Heat from the sun or warm water activates the polymer topcoat
  • 3The topcoat flows back to its original form within minutes
  • 4Surface returns to a perfect, blemish-free finish
Why PPF?

Benefits of Paint Protection Film

PPF is the only protection that physically blocks rock chips, debris, and scratches from ever reaching your paint.

🪨

Rock Chip Defense

The #1 enemy of car paint — rock chips — are completely stopped by PPF's thick urethane layer before they ever touch your paint.

🔄

Self-Healing Surface

Light swirl marks and scratches disappear on their own with heat — your car stays flawless with zero extra effort.

🪟

Virtually Invisible

Modern PPF is optically clear — no one can tell it's there. Your car's paint color and finish show through perfectly.

💧

Hydrophobic Top Coat

PPF's topcoat repels water, bird droppings, bug acids, and road grime — making the vehicle far easier to maintain.

💰

Preserve Resale Value

A chip-free, scratch-free paint job dramatically increases trade-in and private sale value — PPF pays for itself over time.

🔧

Removable & Replaceable

PPF can be safely removed without damaging paint if needed. Individual panels can be replaced — not the whole film.
