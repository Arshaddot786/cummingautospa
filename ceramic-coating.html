How It Works

Our Ceramic Coating Process

Every ceramic coating job follows our strict 6-step process — no shortcuts, no rushing.

1

Inspection

Full vehicle inspection to assess paint condition and note any imperfections before work begins.

2

Wash & Decon

Thorough wash, iron decontamination, and clay bar treatment to remove all surface contaminants.

3

Paint Correction

Machine polishing to remove swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation before coating application.

4

IPA Wipedown

Panel wipe with isopropyl alcohol to remove all polish residue and ensure a perfectly clean surface.

5

Coating Applied

9H ceramic coating applied panel by panel in a climate-controlled environment for perfect bonding.

6

Cure & Inspect

Full cure time respected. Final inspection under LED lighting to ensure flawless results.

Why Ceramic?

Benefits of Ceramic Coating

Wax and sealants wear off in weeks. Ceramic coatings last years — and work harder.

💧

Hydrophobic Effect

Water beads and rolls off effortlessly — taking dirt and grime with it. Your car stays cleaner, longer.

🛡️

9H Scratch Resistance

The hardest consumer-grade coating available. Creates a protective layer over your paint that resists light scratches and swirl marks.

☀️

UV & Oxidation Protection

Blocks UV radiation that causes paint to fade, oxidize, and dull over time — preserving your vehicle's color and finish.

✨

Mirror-Like Gloss

Ceramic coating dramatically enhances paint depth and gloss — your vehicle will look like it just rolled off the showroom floor.

🧼

Easier to Clean

Contaminants can't bond to the coated surface as easily. Washing takes half the time and effort after a ceramic coating.

💰

Protects Resale Value

A well-maintained ceramic-coated vehicle holds its paint condition far better — protecting your investment for years.
